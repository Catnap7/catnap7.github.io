Home Maintenance Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Overview

Home Maintenance Quick is a local-first Android app for tracking recurring home maintenance tasks. The app does not require an account and does not upload your task data to a developer-operated server.

Information You Enter

The app stores the home maintenance tasks you enter on your device. This may include task names, notes, due dates, repeat intervals, priorities, and completion status.

Local Storage

Your home task data is stored locally on your device. You can delete tasks inside the app. You can also remove local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in Android settings.

Advertising

The app may use Google Mobile Ads to show ads. Google may collect or process data according to Google Mobile Ads policies, including device identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, and advertising-related data.

Notifications

Optional Android notifications are used only to remind you about tasks you entered. You can deny or turn off notification permission in Android settings.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell your task data. The developer does not operate a backend service for collecting your task names, notes, or due dates.
