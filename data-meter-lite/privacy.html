Data Meter Lite Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

Data Meter Lite is a local Android utility for viewing live internet speed, tracked device data usage, simple data limits, and optional per-app usage summaries.

Information The App Uses

Data Meter Lite uses Android system network counters to calculate live download/upload speed and local daily/monthly usage totals. These counters are processed on the device.

If you grant Usage Access, Data Meter Lite can read Android usage statistics to show which apps used the most data during the current cycle. Usage Access is optional, and the app remains usable without it.

Information The App Does Not Collect

Data Meter Lite does not require an account, does not upload your usage data to a server, does not inspect the content of your network traffic, and does not run a VPN.

The current MVP release does not include an advertising SDK.

Permissions

  • Notification permission: used to show the live speed foreground notification when you turn on the live meter.
  • Foreground service permission: used only for the user-requested live speed notification.
  • Usage Access: optional, used only to display local per-app data usage summaries.

Data Sharing

Data Meter Lite does not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties.

Data Retention

Usage baselines and settings such as data limits are stored locally on your device. You can remove them by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer account owner through the contact email listed on the Google Play Store page.
