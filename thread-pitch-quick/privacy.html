Thread Pitch Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Thread Pitch Quick is a local-first thread pitch reference calculator. It helps convert pitch and TPI values and compare common Metric, UNC, and UNF references.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store saved pitch checks locally on your device. This data is not sent to an Evmodu server.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not require an account. We do not operate a backend server for saved pitch checks. We do not collect or share personal data, and we do not sell personal data.

Advertising

The current release does not include advertising SDKs and does not use the Android Advertising ID.

Network Access

The current release does not require internet access for its pitch and TPI reference features.

Reference Use

The app provides planning and reference calculations only. Confirm thread requirements with manufacturer specifications, calibrated gauges, or qualified professionals where needed.
