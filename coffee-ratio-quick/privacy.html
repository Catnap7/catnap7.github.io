Coffee Ratio Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Overview

Coffee Ratio Quick is a local-first Android utility for calculating coffee-to-water ratios. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal content.

Information We Collect

The app stores recent brew recipes locally on your device using app storage. This may include coffee amount, water amount, ratio, cups, and selected brew method. This information is not sent to our servers.

Advertising

The app may display ads using Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect device identifiers, approximate location, app interaction data, diagnostics, and other data as described in Google's advertising policies. You can learn more at Google Advertising Policies.

Permissions

The app may use internet and network state access for advertising. It does not use the camera, microphone, contacts, photos, precise location, or account permissions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Advertising data may be processed by Google AdMob according to Google's terms and policies.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for Coffee Ratio Quick.
