Fraction Calculator Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Fraction Calculator Quick is a local-first fraction calculator for simplifying fractions, mixed numbers, decimal results, and compact calculation steps.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store recent calculation history on your device so you can reuse or review previous fraction calculations. This information is not sent to an Evmodu server.

Accounts

The app does not require an account.

Permissions

The app does not require camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos, or file permissions for its calculator features.

Advertising

The app may show ads through Google Mobile Ads. Google may process device or advertising data according to its own policies and your device settings.

Data Deletion

You can clear local calculation history inside the app. You can also remove local app data by uninstalling the app.
