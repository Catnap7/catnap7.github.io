Picture Frame Calculator Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

Overview

Picture Frame Calculator is a local Android utility for calculating picture-frame, mat board, glass, backing, rabbet opening, and cut-list dimensions. The app does not require an account.

Information We Collect

Picture Frame Calculator does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. Calculations are performed on your device.

Local Worksheet

The app does not persist the current worksheet after it closes. Clear removes the values shown in the current session.

Clipboard

The app writes a cut-plan summary to the system clipboard only after you explicitly tap Copy cut plan. Android and other apps may be able to access clipboard content according to your device settings.

Advertising

The app does not include an advertising SDK and does not show ads.

Permissions

The app does not request camera, microphone, contacts, photos, files, location, notification, account, network, or advertising ID permissions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information and do not share personal information with third parties.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
