Stock Count Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Stock Count Quick is a local-first Android app for counting stock items, low-stock thresholds, reorder flags, and simple shelf or supply lists.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store stock item names, quantities, units, areas, low-stock thresholds, reorder flags, and related local settings on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require an account. We do not operate a backend server for your stock list. We do not upload your stock items, quantities, or reorder list to an Evmodu server.

Advertising

The app may show ads through Google Mobile Ads. Google may process device or advertising data according to its own policies and your device settings.

Clipboard

The app writes a stock summary to the clipboard only when you tap the copy summary action.

Data Deletion

You can clear local stock data in the app. You can also delete local app data by clearing app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Permissions

The app uses Internet access to load ads. It does not request camera, location, contacts, microphone, or storage permission.
