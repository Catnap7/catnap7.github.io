BTU Size Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Overview

BTU Size Quick is a local-first Android calculator for estimating room air conditioner sizing. The app does not require an account and does not upload your room measurements to an Evmodu-operated server.

Local Data

The app may save recent BTU estimates locally on your device so you can reuse or copy them later. You can remove this data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Ads

BTU Size Quick may show ads using Google AdMob. Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect or process data such as advertising identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, device identifiers, and approximate device information to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Permissions

The app uses Internet and network state access for ads. It does not request camera, contacts, microphone, precise location, storage, SMS, calendar, or health permissions.

Estimator Notice

BTU Size Quick provides estimates for everyday planning. It does not provide professional HVAC, engineering, legal, or safety advice. Always check manufacturer guidance or a qualified installer before buying or installing equipment.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for BTU Size Quick.
