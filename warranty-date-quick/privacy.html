Warranty Date Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Overview

Warranty Date Quick is a local-first Android app for tracking product warranty end dates. The app does not require an account and does not upload your warranty date list to a developer-operated server.

Information You Enter

The app lets you enter product names, store or category notes, warranty end dates, priority, completion state, and notification schedule state. This information is stored locally on your device.

Local Storage

Your warranty date information stays on your device. You can delete local app data by clearing app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Advertising

The app may use Google Mobile Ads to show ads. Google may collect or process data according to Google Mobile Ads policies, including device identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, and advertising-related data.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app may schedule local Android notifications for warranty date reminders. Notifications are optional and can be managed in Android settings.

Permissions

The app uses Internet access to load ads and may request notification permission for optional reminders. It does not request camera, location, contacts, microphone, or storage permission.

Disclaimer

Warranty Date Quick is for personal date organization only. It does not provide repair, claim, legal, insurance, manufacturer, or professional advice.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell your warranty date data. The developer does not operate a backend service for collecting your warranty date list.
