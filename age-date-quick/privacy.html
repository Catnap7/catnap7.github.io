Age Date Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Age Date Quick is a local-first age and date calculator. The app lets you enter dates and optional notes to calculate exact age, date difference, weekdays, and next birthday timing.

Information Stored On Your Device

Age Date Quick may store recent date calculations and settings on your device. This can include start dates, target dates, optional notes, and creation timestamps.

The app does not require an account and does not upload this date calculation information to a server operated by us.

Advertising

Age Date Quick may show ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or process device information, identifiers, diagnostics, and ad interaction data according to Google's policies. You can review Google's advertising privacy information at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Permissions

The app is designed without calendar, contacts, location, camera, microphone, health, or file permissions.

Calculator Only

Age Date Quick provides date calculations only. It does not provide legal, medical, astrology, or eligibility advice.
