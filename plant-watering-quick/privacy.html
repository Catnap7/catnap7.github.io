Privacy Policy - Plant Watering Quick

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Local Plant Reminder Data

Plant Watering Quick stores the plant watering reminders you enter on your device. This may include plant names, room or location notes, due dates, repeat intervals, priorities, and completion status.

No Account Or Developer Server

The app does not require an account and does not upload your plant reminder data to a developer-operated server.

Advertising

The app may use Google Mobile Ads to show ads. Google may collect or process data according to Google Mobile Ads policies, including device identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, and advertising-related data.

Notifications

Optional Android notifications are used only to remind you about plants you entered.

Deleting Data

You can delete plants inside the app. You can also remove local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in Android settings.
