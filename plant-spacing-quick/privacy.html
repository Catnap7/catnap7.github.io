Plant Spacing Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Overview

Plant Spacing Quick is a local-first Android utility for estimating garden bed rows, plant counts, spacing, buying quantity, and simple cost totals. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal content to the developer.

Information We Collect

The developer does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, photos, files, or garden estimate inputs on a developer server.

Calculator values and recent planting summaries may be stored locally on your device so you can reuse them later. This local data is not sent to Evmodu servers.

Ads

This release of Plant Spacing Quick does not include advertising SDKs and does not show ads.

Permissions

This release does not request internet, network-state, camera, microphone, contacts, location, SMS, phone, calendar, or storage/media permissions.

Data Deletion

Because Plant Spacing Quick does not create an account or store your estimates on a developer server, there is no server-side account data to delete. You can remove local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for Plant Spacing Quick.
