Tile Calculator Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Overview

Tile Calculator Quick is a local-first Android utility for estimating tile quantities, boxes, waste, grout line length, cut-edge tiles, room area, coverage, and material cost. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal content.

Information We Collect

Tile Calculator Quick does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. Calculations are performed on your device.

Advertising

The first release does not include an advertising SDK and does not show ads.

Permissions

The app does not request camera, microphone, contacts, photos, files, location, notification, account, or other sensitive runtime permissions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information and do not share personal information with third parties.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for Tile Calculator Quick.
