Miter Angle Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Miter Angle Quick is a local-first calculator for miter saw settings, cut-line angles, stock offsets, and basic material length planning. The app does not require an account and does not send calculator entries to a server.

Information We Collect

Miter Angle Quick does not collect, sell, or share personal information. Calculator values and recent saved layouts stay on your device using local app storage.

Local Storage

The app may store recent layout results on your device so you can review quick checks later. You can remove this information by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Network, Ads, and Analytics

The current release does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, remote accounts, push notifications, or server sync.

Permissions

The app is designed as an offline calculator and does not request sensitive Android permissions.

Safety Note

Miter Angle Quick is for layout planning. Verify your saw scale, blade kerf, material condition, project plans, local codes, installation instructions, and tool safety guidance before cutting.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
