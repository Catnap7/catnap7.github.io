Pantry Stock Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Overview

Pantry Stock Quick is a local household stock tracker. The app lets you save pantry, fridge, freezer, and household item details on your device.

Information You Enter

The app may store item names, storage locations, quantities, unit labels, expiry dates, and low-stock thresholds locally on your device.

This information is not sent to an app-operated server.

Accounts

The app does not require account registration or login.

Advertising

The initial release does not show ads and does not include an advertising SDK.

Permissions

The app does not require internet, camera, photo, location, contacts, microphone, or notification permissions in the initial release.

Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored pantry data by clearing the app data from Android system settings or using the app reset or clear action where available.
