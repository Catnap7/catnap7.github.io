Stud Count Quick Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Stud Count Quick is a local-first Android app for estimating wall studs, plate boards, waste, and simple layout marks. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Stud Count Quick does not collect personal information. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, files, microphone, camera, or payment information.

Local App Data

The app may save recent copied stud count summaries on your device using Android local storage. This information stays on your device and is used only to show recent local counts inside the app.

Clipboard

When you choose to copy a result, Stud Count Quick places the selected estimate text on your device clipboard. Clipboard handling is controlled by Android and any apps you choose to paste into.

Network, Analytics, and Advertising

Stud Count Quick does not use a backend server, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, or third-party login. The app does not send calculation inputs or results to us.

Children's Privacy

Stud Count Quick is a general utility app and is not directed to children. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Deleting Local Data

You can delete local app data by clearing the app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Planning Disclaimer

Stud Count Quick provides planning estimates for simple wall framing material counts. Always confirm local codes, load paths, openings, corners, connectors, blocking, material specifications, and safe measurement practices before cutting or building.
