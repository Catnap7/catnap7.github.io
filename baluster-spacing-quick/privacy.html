Baluster Spacing Quick Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Baluster Spacing Quick is a local-first Android app for estimating equal baluster gaps, center spacing, and first center marks. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Baluster Spacing Quick does not collect personal information. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, files, microphone, camera, or payment information.

Local App Data

The app may save recent copied baluster spacing summaries on your device using Android local storage. This information stays on your device and is used only to show recent local layouts inside the app.

Clipboard

When you choose to copy a result, Baluster Spacing Quick places the selected layout text on your device clipboard. Clipboard handling is controlled by Android and any apps you choose to paste into.

Network, Analytics, and Advertising

Baluster Spacing Quick does not use a backend server, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, or third-party login. The app does not send calculation inputs or results to us.

Children's Privacy

Baluster Spacing Quick is a general utility app and is not directed to children. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Deleting Local Data

You can delete local app data by clearing the app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Planning Disclaimer

Baluster Spacing Quick provides planning estimates for simple railing layouts. Always confirm local codes, guardrail requirements, stair angles, openings, connectors, material specifications, and safe measurement practices before cutting or building.
