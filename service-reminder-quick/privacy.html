Service Reminder Quick Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Service Reminder Quick is a local-first vehicle service reminder app.

Information You Enter

The app lets you enter service names, vehicle or note text, optional mileage notes, due dates, priorities, and completion status. This information is stored locally on your device.

Accounts And Cloud

The app does not require an account and does not provide cloud sync. The app developer does not operate a backend database for your service reminder data.

Advertising

The production version of the app does not show ads and does not include an advertising SDK.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app may schedule local Android notifications for service reminders. Notifications are optional.

Permissions

The app may request notification permission. The production version does not request internet access.
