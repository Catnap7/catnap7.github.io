Ohm Law Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Ohm Law Quick is a local-first calculator for voltage, current, resistance, and power.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app can save recent calculation results locally on your device. This information is not sent to an Evmodu server.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not require an account. We do not operate a backend server for saved calculations. We do not collect, share, or sell personal data.

Advertising

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not show ads.

Permissions

The Android release does not request internet or network-state permissions. The calculator works locally on your device.

Reference Use

The app is intended as an educational and planning reference. Always verify real circuits with rated components, instruments, and applicable safety rules.
