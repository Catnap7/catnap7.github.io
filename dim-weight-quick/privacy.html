Dim Weight Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Overview

Dim Weight Quick is a local-first Android utility for calculating dimensional, volumetric, and billable shipping weight estimates. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal content to the developer.

Information We Collect

The app stores recent shipment estimates locally on your device using app storage. This may include shipment names, package dimensions, actual weight, quantity, divisor, and calculated estimates. This information is not sent to our servers.

Advertising

This release does not include advertising SDKs and does not display ads.

Permissions

The app does not request internet, network state, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, precise location, health data, or account permissions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information and do not share your locally stored shipment estimates with third parties.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for Dim Weight Quick.
