Discount Calculator Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Discount Calculator Quick is a local-first shopping calculator. The app helps you calculate sale prices, savings, optional stacked discounts, tax, currency display, and rounding settings.

Information Stored On Your Device

Discount Calculator Quick may store recent calculations and settings on your device. This can include prices, discount percentages, tax settings, currency symbol, and rounding mode.

The app does not require an account and does not upload this calculation information to a server operated by us.

Advertising

This release does not show ads and does not include an advertising SDK.

Permissions

The release build does not request camera, contacts, location, microphone, health, file, advertising ID, or Internet permissions.

Data Deletion

You can clear recent calculations inside the app. Uninstalling the app also removes local app data from your device.
