Tip Split Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Overview

Tip Split Quick is a local-first Android calculator for tips and bill splitting. The app does not require an account and does not upload your bill-splitting data to a developer-operated server.

Information You Enter

The app may save recent calculations locally on your device, such as bill amount, tip percent, service charge, number of people, total amount, per-person amount, currency symbol, and timestamp.

Local Storage

Your recent calculation history stays on your device. You can delete local app data by clearing app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Advertising

The app may use Google Mobile Ads to show ads. Google may collect or process data according to Google Mobile Ads policies, including device identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, and advertising-related data.

Permissions

The app uses Internet access to load ads. It does not request camera, location, contacts, microphone, storage, or notification permission.

Financial Disclaimer

Tip Split Quick is a calculator for everyday convenience. It does not provide financial, tax, legal, or payment advice. Always verify the final amount on your receipt or payment terminal.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell your bill-splitting data. The developer does not operate a backend service for collecting your calculation history.
