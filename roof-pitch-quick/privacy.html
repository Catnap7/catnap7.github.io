Roof Pitch Quick Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Roof Pitch Quick is a local-first Android app for calculating roof pitch, angle, rafter length, slope factor, and related planning values. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Roof Pitch Quick does not collect personal information. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, files, microphone, camera, or payment information.

Local App Data

The app may save recent copied roof pitch calculation summaries on your device using Android local storage. This information stays on your device and is used only to show your recent local results inside the app.

Clipboard

When you choose to copy a result, Roof Pitch Quick places the selected calculation text on your device clipboard. Clipboard handling is controlled by Android and any apps you choose to paste into.

Network, Analytics, and Advertising

Roof Pitch Quick does not use a backend server, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, or third-party login. The app does not send calculation inputs or results to us.

Children's Privacy

Roof Pitch Quick is a general utility app and is not directed to children. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Deleting Local Data

You can delete local app data by clearing the app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Planning Disclaimer

Roof Pitch Quick provides planning calculations for simple right-triangle roof pitch scenarios. Always confirm local codes, framing details, overhangs, ridge boards, material specifications, and safe measurement practices before cutting or building.
