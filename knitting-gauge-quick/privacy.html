Knitting Gauge Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Overview

Knitting Gauge Quick is a local-first Android utility for estimating cast-on stitches, row counts, repeat rounding, and finished-size values from a knitting swatch. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal content to the developer.

Information We Collect

The developer does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, photos, files, or knitting project inputs on a developer server.

Calculator values and recent gauge summaries may be stored locally on your device so you can reuse them later. This local data is not sent to Evmodu servers.

Ads

The app includes Google Mobile Ads integration for future ad-supported releases. When production ads are enabled, Google may process ad-related identifiers, diagnostics, and delivery data under Google's policies. Knitting Gauge Quick does not send your calculator inputs to the developer for ad targeting.

Permissions

The app may use internet and network-state access for ad loading. It does not request camera, microphone, contacts, location, SMS, phone, calendar, or storage/media permissions.

Data Deletion

Because Knitting Gauge Quick does not create an account or store your estimates on a developer server, there is no server-side account data to delete. You can remove local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for Knitting Gauge Quick.
