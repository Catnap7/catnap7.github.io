Belt Length Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Belt Length Quick is a local-first pulley belt calculator. The app lets you enter pulley diameters, center distance, and driver RPM to calculate belt length and related reference values.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store saved calculation history locally on your device. This data is not sent to an Evmodu server.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not require an account. We do not operate a backend server for saved belt calculations. We do not sell personal data.

Advertising

The app includes Google Mobile Ads for advertising. Google may collect and process data for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and diagnostics according to Google's policies and your device settings.

Planning Reference

The app provides planning calculations only. Confirm belt and pulley requirements with manufacturer data or a qualified professional before installation or operation.
