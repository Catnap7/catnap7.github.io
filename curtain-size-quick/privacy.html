Curtain Size Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Overview

Curtain Size Quick is a local-first Android utility for estimating curtain rod width, panel length, fullness, custom panel width, and ready-made panel quantity. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal content to the developer.

Information We Collect

The developer does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, photos, files, or window measurement inputs on a developer server.

Calculator values and recent curtain summaries may be stored locally on your device so you can reuse them later. This local data is not sent to Evmodu servers.

Ads

This release does not include ads and does not include an advertising SDK. Curtain Size Quick does not send calculator inputs to the developer or to an advertising service.

Permissions

This release does not request internet, network-state, camera, microphone, contacts, location, SMS, phone, calendar, or storage/media permissions.

Data Deletion

Because Curtain Size Quick does not create an account or store your estimates on a developer server, there is no server-side account data to delete. You can remove local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for Curtain Size Quick.
