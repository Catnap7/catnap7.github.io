Random Number Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Random Number Quick is a local-first utility app for generating random numbers, dice rolls, and coin flips.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store your last settings and recent result history on your device. This data is used only to make the app easier to use.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require an account. We do not operate a backend server for generated results. We do not upload generated results to an Evmodu server.

Advertising

The app may show ads through Google Mobile Ads. Google may process device or advertising data according to its own policies and your device settings.

Clipboard

The app writes to the clipboard only when you tap the copy button.
