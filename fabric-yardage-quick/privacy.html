Fabric Yardage Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Overview

Fabric Yardage Quick is a local-first Android utility for estimating fabric yardage for sewing, quilting, and craft projects. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal content to the developer.

Information We Collect

The app stores recent yardage estimates locally on your device using app storage. This may include project names, fabric width, piece dimensions, quantity, allowance percentage, and calculated yardage. This information is not sent to our servers.

Advertising

This release does not display ads and does not include an advertising SDK.

Permissions

This release does not request internet or network state permissions. It does not use the camera, microphone, contacts, photos, precise location, health data, or account permissions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. The app does not send recent estimates to developer servers or share them with third parties.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for Fabric Yardage Quick.
