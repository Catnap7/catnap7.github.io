Arc Segment Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Arc Segment Quick is a local-first calculator for arc length, chord length, segment height, sector area, segment area, and arc plus chord perimeter. The app does not require an account and does not send calculator entries to a server.

Information We Collect

Arc Segment Quick does not collect, sell, or share personal information. Calculator values and recent saved arc checks stay on your device using local app storage.

Local Storage

The app may store recent arc results on your device so you can review quick checks later. You can remove this information by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Clipboard

When you tap the copy button, the app places the calculated result text on your device clipboard. Clipboard behavior is handled by Android.

Network, Ads, and Analytics

The current release does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, remote accounts, push notifications, or server sync.

Permissions

The app is designed as an offline calculator and does not request sensitive Android permissions.

Safety Note

Arc Segment Quick is for circular layout planning. Verify drawing scale, material thickness, tool kerf, tolerance, setup, and project requirements before cutting, bending, or machining.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
