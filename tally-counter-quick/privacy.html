Tally Counter Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Tally Counter Quick is a local-first counting utility. The app does not require an account and does not upload your counters to our servers.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store counter names, values, step sizes, and target values locally on your device.

Advertising

The app may show ads using Google AdMob. Google Mobile Ads may collect or process device identifiers, app interactions, diagnostics, and related advertising data according to Google's policies.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or account credentials.

Data Deletion

You can delete counters inside the app or clear app data from Android system settings.
