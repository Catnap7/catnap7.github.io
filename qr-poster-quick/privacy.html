QR Poster Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Overview

QR Poster Quick is a local QR creation utility for URL, text, and Wi-Fi QR cards.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store recent QR payload summaries on your device so you can reuse recent QR cards. This information stays on your device and is not sent to Evmodu servers.

Accounts

The app does not require an account.

QR Payloads

QR codes are generated locally on your device. Wi-Fi QR payloads may include passwords, so clear history if you do not want those payloads stored locally.

Ads

The production release does not show ads and does not include an advertising SDK.

Permissions

The app does not require camera, location, contacts, microphone, photos, files, notification, network, or advertising ID permissions.

Local Data Deletion

You can clear recent QR payload history inside the app or by clearing app storage from Android settings.
