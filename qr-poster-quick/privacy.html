QR Poster Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

Overview

QR Poster Quick is a local QR creation utility for URL, text, and Wi-Fi QR cards.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store up to eight recent non-sensitive QR entries on your device so you can reuse recent QR cards. This information stays on your device and is not sent to Evmodu or any third-party server.

Accounts

The app does not require an account.

QR Payloads

QR codes are generated locally on your device. A password-bearing Wi-Fi QR payload is available only during the current session and is never saved to history. On first load, the app also removes any password-bearing Wi-Fi entries that may have been saved by an older version.

Clipboard

The app writes a QR payload to the system clipboard only when you explicitly choose Copy. If you copy a Wi-Fi QR payload, the clipboard content may include the password you entered. Android and other apps may be able to access clipboard content according to your device settings.

Ads

The production release does not show ads and does not include an advertising SDK.

Permissions

The app does not require camera, location, contacts, microphone, photos, files, notification, network, or advertising ID permissions.

Local Data Deletion

You can delete individual history entries or clear all history inside the app. The app briefly offers Undo after either action. You can also permanently remove local app data by clearing app storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.
