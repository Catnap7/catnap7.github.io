Recipe Scale Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Overview

Recipe Scale Quick is a local-first Android utility for scaling recipe ingredient amounts by servings or direct multiplier. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal content to the developer.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from Recipe Scale Quick. The app stores recent scale plans locally on your device using app storage. This may include ingredient names, ingredient amounts, units, serving counts, and multipliers. This information is not sent to our servers.

Advertising

This release does not display ads and does not include an advertising SDK.

Permissions

The app does not request internet, network state, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, precise location, health data, or account permissions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information and do not share app data with third parties.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing for Recipe Scale Quick.
