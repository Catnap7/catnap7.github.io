Flooring Box Quick Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Flooring Box Quick is a local-first Android app for estimating flooring boxes, waste, underlayment, and cost. The app does not collect, share, sell, or transmit personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, device identifiers, location, photos, contacts, or any other user data.

Local App Data

The app may store recent flooring estimates locally on your device so you can review previous calculations. This local information stays on your device and is not sent to us or to any server.

Clipboard

When you tap the copy button, the app places the estimate text on your device clipboard. Clipboard behavior is handled by Android.

Network, Ads, and Accounts

  • The release app has no internet or network feature.
  • The app does not show ads.
  • The app does not use analytics SDKs.
  • The app does not require an account or sign-in.

Children's Privacy

The app is a general utility and does not knowingly collect information from children or any other users.

Data Deletion

Because we do not collect data, we do not maintain server-side user data to delete. You can remove local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in Android settings.
