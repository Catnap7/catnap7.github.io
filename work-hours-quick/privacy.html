Work Hours Quick Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Work Hours Quick is a local-first work hours calculator. The app lets you enter shift times, unpaid breaks, hourly rate, overtime settings, and optional notes to calculate estimated gross pay.

Information Stored On Your Device

Work Hours Quick may store recent shift records and settings on your device. This can include dates, start and end times, break minutes, hourly rate, overtime settings, and optional notes.

The app does not require an account and does not upload this shift information to a server operated by us.

Advertising

Work Hours Quick may show ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or process device information, identifiers, diagnostics, and ad interaction data according to Google's policies. You can review Google's advertising privacy information at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Permissions

The app is designed without location, contacts, microphone, camera, calendar, health, or file permissions.

Estimates Only

Work Hours Quick provides estimated work-hour and gross-pay calculations only. It is not payroll, tax, legal, or employment advice.
